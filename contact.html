what is an artistree?

ArtistreeApp is a location-based check-in app for art galleries

User's "check-in" automatically once they enter an art gallery region, galleries gather attendance metrics, users get information custom tailored to their current location and preferred art medium: sculpture, portrait, painting, photography, audio-visual, mixed media etc...
